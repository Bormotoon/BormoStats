# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, direct, and professional.
- Focus discussion on facts, tradeoffs, and reproducible behavior.
- Assume good intent, but keep the technical bar high.
- Welcome corrections and review feedback without hostility.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Trolling, baiting, or intentionally derailing technical discussion
- Discriminatory or demeaning language

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates these expectations.
Repeated or severe violations may lead to removal from collaboration spaces.
